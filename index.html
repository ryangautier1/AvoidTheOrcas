How to Play
Click in any square to reveal whether there is an orca in that square. If there is not, a number will appear showing how many orcas are in squares adjacent to that square. If there are no orcas in the adjacent squares either, those squares' contents are automatically revealed!

Click squares until all orca-free squares are revealed. If you click a square with an orca, it's game over!

You may right-click any square to flag it. Right-click again to remove the flag. You have as many flags as there are orcas. Use this tool to keep track of where the orcas are.
